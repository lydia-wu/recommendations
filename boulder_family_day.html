Chautauqua Park & The Flatirons
900 Baseline Rd, Boulder · 2 glorious hours

The Flatirons rise directly in front of you. Enormous open meadow. General Store has ice cream. The toddler runs free, you get the view, and he gets the mountains calling his name. This is the heart of the day.

2 hrs Most iconic spot in Colorado Paid parking Saturdays — $2.50/hr
For your 21-month-old
  • Bubbles in the meadow — they'll chase them for 20 solid minutes
  • Walk barefoot in the grass — pure sensory bliss
  • Find a stick. This becomes their most prized possession for an hour.
  • Stack rocks near the trailhead — toddlers are obsessed with this
  • Ice cream cone at the General Store as the grand finale 🍦
For the adventurous husband
  • Scramble base of First Flatiron — 20-30 min, no gear needed, iconic
  • Royal Arch Trail: steep, rocky, natural arch + waterfall at the top
  • Bluebell-Baird Trail: climbs fast with insane exposure views
🅿️Parking — Chautauqua$2.50/hr on Saturdays

Sherpa's Adventure

(303) 440-7151
Sat dinner: 5:00–10:00 PM
Cooking fat: Ghee + charcoal tandoor
Vibe: Converted house with patio, Flatiron views, cozy and family-friendly. Boulder institution since 2002. Every dish made to order from scratch. Voted best Nepalese/Indian restaurant 2002–2025.
Distance: 5 min from Chautauqua
Backup — On your way home
Spice Room
📍 3100 E Colfax Ave, Denver
(303) 285-3700
Hours: Open daily to 10 PM Fri/Sat
Cooking fat: Rice bran oil + ghee, no MSG
Vibe: Denver's #1 rated Indian (4.9★, 3,400+ reviews). On your drive home to Aurora. More casual, faster, 80+ dish menu.
Distance: ~35 min — on the route home
⚠️ If you pick Sherpa's: call ahead at (303) 440-7151 — dinner starts at 5 PM. Arriving at 4:30 is between services. They may seat you with drinks while you wait, or time your Chautauqua departure to arrive closer to 5.
Sherpa's — order this
  • Sherpa Stew (yak or chicken) — the signature dish, deeply nourishing, unlike anything else
  • Steamed Momos — handmade Tibetan dumplings with homemade chutney
  • Saag with lamb or paneer — creamed spinach, fresh garlic, ginger
  • Garlic Naan — tandoor-baked, brushed with fresh ghee at the table
  • Homemade Sherpa Chai to finish — the recipe that launched a national brand
Spice Room — order this

  • Palak Paneer — fresh paneer in emerald spinach
  • Garlic Naan — clay oven, hand-rolled daily
  • Mango Lassi to finish
